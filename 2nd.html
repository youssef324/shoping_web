Welcome to OPIUM! We value your satisfaction and aim to provide a seamless shopping experience. Please review our policies below to ensure clarity and mutual understanding:

Refunds and Replacements

At OPIUM, we prioritize ensuring you find the perfect piece. As such:

  • No Cash Refunds: We do not offer monetary refunds.
  • Replacement Policy: If you are unsatisfied with your purchase, you may exchange it for another item or a different size.
  • Conditions for Replacement:
    • Items must be returned in unused, unworn, and undamaged condition, with all tags and packaging intact.
    • Replacement requests must be made within 14 days of receiving your order.
    • Proof of purchase (e.g., receipt or order confirmation) must accompany the returned item.

Please Note: Availability of specific sizes or designs for exchange depends on stock at the time of request.

Shipping and Returns

  • Customers are responsible for the shipping costs associated with returning items for replacement, unless the product was defective or an error was made on our part.
  • If you receive a defective or incorrect item, notify us within 48 hours of delivery for assistance.

Final Sale Items

  • Items marked as "Final Sale" are not eligible for exchange or replacement.

Cancellations

  • Orders may be canceled only before they are shipped. Once dispatched, the order cannot be canceled and must follow the replacement policy if changes are required.

Promotions and Discounts

  • Items purchased during sales or promotions are subject to the same replacement policy unless stated otherwise. Discounts cannot be carried over to new exchanges.

Custom Orders

  • Custom or personalized items are non-refundable and cannot be exchanged unless they arrive damaged or defective.
